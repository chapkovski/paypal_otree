We (Nuffield CESS) are a group of researchers based in University of Oxford, United Kingdom. We have partnered with an economic research organisation (Oxera) to conduct a study into retirement decisions. The data collected in this survey will only be used for the purposes of the study, and your privacy and anonymity will be maintained.
It is very important for the success of our research project that you complete the survey until the end, once you have started. This survey should take on average about 25 minutes to complete.
Please read the following statements carefully and answer the question below.
Our Commitments and Privacy Policy
 
In this survey, some of retirement scenarios and some of information you are presented with will be hypothetical but designed to be similar to information and situations you could encounter in the real world. This implies:
 
  • We never deceive participants. For example, if we give you a quiz about prior information that has been presented to you, we will only use that prior information to assess whether your answer is correct or not.
  • We keep our promises made to participants. For example, if we promise a certain payment, participants will indeed receive it.
  • In the event that we are responsible for a mistake that is to the disadvantage of participants, we will inform and compensate the respective participants.
  • We design, conduct and report our research in accordance with recognized scientific standards and ethical principles.
  We adhere to the terms of our privacy policy as stated below.
 
  • The data in the participants’ database will only be used for the purpose of the study (to explore retirement decisions)
  • There is no link between the personal data in the participants’ database and the data collected during a study.
  • The generated anonymous data will be used to write a report and to give presentations. The end products will be publicly available.
  • Your participation in this study is purely voluntary, and you may withdraw your participation or your data at any time without any penalty to you. 

Notes: If you have any questions about this study, you may contact us at cess@nuffield.ox.ac.uk
